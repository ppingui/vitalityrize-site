Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2025

VitalityRise is a fully offline, privacy-focused mobile application designed to support men's wellness. We take privacy seriously and ensure that all data remains on your device unless explicitly shared by you.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or health-related information externally. All app activity and tracking are stored securely and privately on your device.

2. No Account Required

VitalityRise does not require any login, account registration, or cloud syncing. Your use is anonymous and fully local.

3. Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use any third-party analytics, ad networks, or tracking technologies. We respect your privacy by design.

4. Optional Email Contact

If you contact us via email at support@vitalityrize.me, we may retain the email solely for the purpose of responding to your request.

5. Your Rights

Since no identifiable data is collected, there is no personal data to access, delete, or export. You may delete the app at any time to erase all locally stored content.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy if needed. All updates will be posted here and effective immediately upon publishing.
